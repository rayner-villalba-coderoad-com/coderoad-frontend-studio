# Senior Frontend Engineer (Level 3)

| Title: | Senior Frontend Engineer (Level 3) |
| --- | --- |
| Department: | Delivery / Frontend Engineering |
| Reports to: | Lead Frontend Engineer |
| Employment Type: | Contractor |

## About the Role

The **Senior Frontend Engineer (Level 3)** is a highly autonomous contributor and a subject matter expert in frontend architecture. At Level 3, the focus shifts from "executing tasks" to **"owning outcomes."** You are responsible for ensuring that our products are not only functional but also scalable, maintainable, and performant.

While this is an individual contributor role, you will serve as a technical mentor to Junior and Intermediate peers, modeling best practices and driving the "how" of technical execution within your POD. You will solve the most complex technical problems and contribute meaningfully to architectural discussions across the frontend guild.

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Technical Execution & Ownership
*   **End-to-End Delivery:** Own the lifecycle of complex frontend features—from initial requirement analysis with designers to production deployment and validation.
*   **System Architecture:** Design and implement scalable state management and component patterns using **React (Hooks, Context API, Redux/Zustand)** and **TypeScript**.
*   **Optimization Specialist:** Proactively identify and resolve performance bottlenecks, accessibility gaps, and SEO requirements within the frontend ecosystem.
*   **Tooling Strategy:** Master and optimize the build pipeline using **Vite**, ensuring efficient development workflows for the entire team.

### 2. Collaboration & Technical Leadership
*   **In-Depth Peer Review:** Provide insightful, architectural-level feedback during code reviews to elevate the team's overall quality and adherence to **Clean Code** principles.
*   **Structured Mentorship:** Support the growth of L1 and L2 engineers through active pair programming, technical documentation, and clear career-path guidance.
*   **Technical Advocacy:** Champion frontend best practices (TDD, Design Systems, Atomic Design) and ensure their consistent application across projects.
*   **Tailwind Mastery:** Enforce high standards for UI consistency and responsiveness using **Tailwind CSS**.

### 3. Quality & Continuous Improvement
*   **Technical Debt Management:** Proactively identify, document, and resolve technical debt to maintain system health and developer velocity.
*   **Innovation:** Research and propose new libraries, methodologies, or tools that can improve the POD's efficiency or product quality.
*   **Clear Documentation:** Ensure technical designs, component libraries, and infrastructure changes are documented for long-term maintainability.

## Success Measures
*   **Autonomy:** Ability to take vague requirements and turn them into high-quality technical realities with minimal supervision.
*   **Review Impact:** Measurable improvement in team output and code quality resulting from your feedback and guidance.
*   **System Reliability:** Consistent delivery of stable, high-performance features that align with client expectations and minimize production defects.
*   **Mentorship ROI:** Success and growth of the Junior and Intermediate engineers under your technical guidance.

## Core Competencies
*   **Frontend Expertise:** Advanced proficiency in **React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS**. Deep understanding of the web platform and browser internals.
*   **Analytical Thinking:** Ability to break down complex systems and understand the downstream impacts of technical changes.
*   **Leadership Mindset:** High sense of ownership, **coachability** at a senior level, and accountability for the POD's technical success.
*   **Advanced English:** Strong ability to articulate technical concepts and architectural trade-offs to international clients and stakeholders.

## Requirements
*   Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent seniority).
*   **5+ years** of professional experience in frontend development.
*   Proven track record of delivering scalable, high-impact technical solutions in Agile environments.
*   Deep understanding of CI/CD, Git workflows, and modern deployment strategies.
*   **Mandatory:** A strong portfolio demonstrating architectural ownership of complex React-based platforms.

## What you’ll love:
*   USA Contractor / 100% Remote / Paid in USD.
*   High-impact leadership opportunities within global projects.
*   Mentorship-first culture focused on senior career progression into Lead or Architect roles.
*   Generous PTO and flexible work-life balance.
