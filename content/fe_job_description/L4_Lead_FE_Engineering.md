# Lead Frontend Engineer (Level 4)

| --- | --- |

| Reports to: | Architect / Manager, Engineering |
| Employment Type: | Contractor |

## About the Role

The **Lead Frontend Engineer (Level 4)** is a tactical technical leader and the **"Technical Anchor"** for a specific POD or project team. In this role, you bridge the gap between high-level architectural requirements and daily execution, ensuring the success of your team through hands-on leadership and technical excellence.

This is a hands-on role (approx. 60-70% coding and peer reviewing) where you will lead a team of Senior, Intermediate, and Junior developers. You are responsible for team velocity, code quality, and developing an incipient understanding of how frontend decisions impact our clients' business goals.

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Tactical Leadership & POD Success
*   **Team Guidance:** Direct daily technical activities of the frontend POD, ensuring clear task distribution and unblocking team members through active problem-solving.
*   **Performance & Growth:** Conduct scheduled 1:1 coaching sessions and lead official performance reviews, providing evidence-based, constructive feedback to drive team growth.
*   **Morale & Culture:** Monitor team mood, proactively identifying burnout risks while fostering a high-performance environment rooted in **coachability** and a **"clean code" mindset**.
*   **Hands-on Participation:** Lead by example by delivering complex features, resolving critical bug fixes, and maintaining an active role in the codebase using **React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS**.

### 2. Client Partnership & Strategy
*   **Technical Advisor:** Act as the primary technical point of contact for clients, translating business challenges into actionable frontend roadmaps and **Vite**-powered build strategies.
*   **Expectation Management:** Manage technical expectations regarding timelines, risks, and UI/UX trade-offs, ensuring transparency and trust with all stakeholders.
*   **Implementation Design:** Design technical components and modules based on high-level architecture, ensuring scalability and maintainability across the account.

### 3. Quality & Process Ownership
*   **Review Authority:** Own the local code review process, ensuring strict adherence to the project’s engineering standards and best practices.
*   **Sprint Predictability:** Break down complex features into manageable technical tasks, providing realistic estimations and collaborating with Project Managers to ensure sprint goals are technically sound.
*   **Risk Mitigation:** Identify and escalate potential technical bottlenecks or risks at the project level before they impact the delivery schedule.

## Success Measures
*   **Client & Team Satisfaction (NPS):** Maintaining high technical satisfaction scores from both client stakeholders and internal team members.
*   **Sprint Velocity:** Consistently meeting sprint commitments with high predictability and minimal carry-over.
*   **Team Autonomy:** Measurable improvement in the skill level and independence of the engineers within your POD.
*   **Code Health:** Low production defect rates and high adherence to linting, testing, and accessibility standards.

## Core Competencies
*   **Technical Excellence:** Mastery of **React (Hooks/Context)**, **TypeScript**, **Tailwind**, and stable delivery using **Vite**.
*   **Leadership & Coaching:** Proven ability to guide others, foster a collaborative environment, and handle interpersonal conflicts effectively.
*   **Execution Focus:** A strong drive to "get things done" without compromising on technical quality or architectural integrity.
*   **English/Spanish Fluency:** Bilingual proficiency is mandatory for effectively bridging client communication and internal team coordination.

## Requirements
*   Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
*   **4+ years** of professional experience in frontend engineering, with at least **1 year** in a lead or "anchor" role.
*   Strong experience in Agile/Scrum environments and managing diverse technical teams.
*   **Bilingual mandatory:** Fluency in English and Spanish.

## What you’ll love:
*   USA Contractor / 100% Remote / Paid in USD.
*   A fast-paced leadership role where you influence both technical strategy and client success.
*   Direct impact on team growth through a dedicated mentorship framework.
*   Comprehensive PTO and support for health assistance programs.
