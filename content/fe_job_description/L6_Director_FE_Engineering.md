# Director of Frontend Engineering (Level 6)

| Title: | Director of Frontend Engineering (Level 6) |
| --- | --- |
| Department: | Delivery / Frontend Engineering |
| Reports to: | VP, Engineering & Delivery |
| Employment Type: | Contractor |

## About the Role

The **Director of Frontend Engineering (Level 6)** is responsible for leading, scaling, and ensuring the excellence of the entire Frontend organization at CodeRoad. This role bridges strategic technical leadership, organizational growth, and delivery oversight, while fostering a culture of innovation, high-level **coachability**, and technical rigor.

As a strategic technical partner for our clients, you will align their long-term business goals with our engineering capabilities. You are a **"Trusted Technology Advisor"** who ensures that CodeRoad remains at the forefront of frontend innovation while maintaining operational excellence and a Relentless **"Clean Code"** culture across all teams.

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Strategic Client Partnership & Advisory
*   **Executive Liaison:** Act as the primary technical liaison for key global accounts, ensuring frontend solutions are aligned with strategic business objectives.
*   **Solution Leadership:** Guide high-level architectural choices (e.g., micro-frontends, design system scaling) to ensure long-term stability using **React, TypeScript, and Vite**.
*   **Thought Leadership:** Drive technical credibility during pre-sales, proposals, and PoCs, proactively identifying opportunities for technological transformation and ROI for the client.

### 2. Organizational Leadership & People Development
*   **Executive Mentorship:** Coach and mentor Studio Leads, Architects, and Level 5 Managers, shaping the next generation of technical leaders.
*   **Career Frameworks:** Partner with the VP and People teams to implement and refine career paths for all frontend roles (L1-L5).
*   **High Performance Culture:** Foster absolute accountability and professional growth, modeling high **coachability** and a proactive communication style for the entire department.

### 3. Delivery Oversight & Quality Governance
*   **Excellence at Scale:** Define and enforce corporate-wide coding standards, quality frameworks, and accessibility guidelines using **Tailwind CSS** and modern testing protocols.
*   **KPI Management:** Oversee departmental KPIs (velocity, quality, utilization) and address systematic risks across frontend PODs.
*   **Hands-on Grounding:** Maintain a strong connection to the codebase by participating in high-stakes code reviews and occasionally delivering small features or bug fixes to model **"Clean Code"** best practices.

### 4. Continuous Innovation & Process
*   **Tech Stack Evolution:** Evaluate emerging technologies and their relevance to CodeRoad’s service offerings, ensuring our stack (React, TS, Vite) remains industry-leading.
*   **Agile Maturity:** Drive consistency and maturity in Agile engineering methodologies across all project teams.
*   **Cross-Functional Alignment:** Partner with Sales, Product, and Talent leaders to ensure our frontend capabilities meet market demands and client needs.

## Success Measures
*   **Strategic Client Satisfaction (NPS):** Maintaining top-tier technical satisfaction scores from executive client stakeholders.
*   **Organizational Health (eNPS):** Maintaining low attrition and high employee engagement across the frontend organization.
*   **Technical Stability:** Successful reduction of systemic technical debt and zero critical production failures across accounts.
*   **Global Delivery Success:** On-time and on-budget execution of high-impact milestones as defined in client SOWs.

## Core Competencies
*   **Strategic Client Partnership:** Ability to connect technology services to business ROI and long-term client success.
*   **Organizational Scaling:** Proven ability to build and lead high-performing engineering teams in a nearshore/offshore model.
*   **Communication & Influence:** Masterful negotiation and the ability to demonstrate credibility and thought leadership to stakeholders.
*   **Advanced Web Architecture:** Deep expertise in the modern web platform, frontend ecosystems, and scalable infrastructure.
*   **Bilingual mandatory:** Advanced fluency in English and Spanish for global stakeholder management.

## Requirements
*   Bachelor’s or Advanced Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
*   Proven experience as a **senior technical leader** or consultant (Director/VP level) in technology services.
*   Deep expertise across multiple frontend platforms and modern delivery lifecycles.
*   Strong track record of mentoring leaders and driving engineering culture.

*   A professional environment focused on continuous learning and mentorship at the executive level.
