# Architect / Manager, Frontend Engineering (Level 5)

| Title: | Architect / Manager, Frontend Engineering (Level 5) |
| --- | --- |
| Department: | Delivery / Frontend Engineering |
| Reports to: | Engineering Director |
| Employment Type: | Contractor |

## About the Role

The **Architect / Manager of Frontend Engineering (Level 5)** is a high-level technical leader at CodeRoad, focused on business impact and client success. This role bridges the gap between the technical vision and the client’s commercial objectives, acting as a strategic consultant and ensuring operational excellence in delivery across one or more accounts.

This is a hybrid role that combines **Solution Architecture**, **Strategic Account Management**, and **Team Leadership**. While highly strategic, you remain technically anchored, ensuring CodeRoad’s engineering standards are rigorously applied while also contributing hands-on to critical features and modeling "clean code" excellence for the entire organization.

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Strategic Account & Client Management
*   **Technical Partnership:** Act as the primary technical point of contact for assigned clients, translating their business challenges into robust frontend roadmaps using **React, TypeScript, and Vite**.
*   **Client Advisor:** Proactively identify opportunities for technological improvement or infrastructure modernization (e.g., migrating to **Tailwind CSS**, optimizing build pipelines) to add business value.
*   **Expectation Management:** Manage high-level technical expectations, ensuring transparency regarding architectural risks, delivery timelines, and ROI on technical decisions.

### 2. Solution Architecture & Technical Oversight
*   **Architectural Leadership:** Define and oversee the frontend architecture of assigned projects, ensuring they are scalable, secure, and maintainable.
*   **Quality Governance:** Enforce rigorous engineering standards. Perform critical code reviews and periodic architectural audits to ensure high-performance and accessible outcomes.
*   **Tactical Execution:** Lead by example by tackling high-complexity features, critical bug fixes, and performance optimizations using modern hooks and context patterns.

### 3. Delivery Excellence & Engineering Management
*   **Team Leadership:** Oversee the technical execution of one or more frontend PODs, ensuring efficient workflows and meeting defined velocity and quality KPIs.
*   **Coaching & Performance:** Conduct mandatory 1:1 sessions (bi-weekly/monthly) and lead official performance evaluations for your team, fostering a culture of high **coachability** and rapid growth.
*   **Agile Orchestration:** Partner with Project Managers to plan sprints, estimate multi-team efforts, and prioritize the technical backlog according to business needs.
*   **Resource Optimization:** Ensure all frontend engineers are performing at their peak technical capacity and have the necessary tools for project success.

### 4. Technical Pre-Sales & Advisory
*   **Solution Design:** Support the Sales/Solutions team in creating technical proposals, high-level estimates, and initial architecture designs for new prospects.
*   **Advisory Services:** Provide high-level technical consultancy to clients regarding frontend ecosystems, micro-frontends, and modern web platforms.

## Success Measures
*   **Client Satisfaction (NPS):** Maintaining high technical satisfaction scores from primary client stakeholders.
*   **Team Health (eNPS):** Maintaining low attrition and high technical satisfaction scores within your assigned team.
*   **Outcome Stability:** Successful completion of project milestones on-time, on-budget, and with high technical quality.
*   **Sprint Predictability:** Meeting sprint commitments with minimal carry-over and low defect rates in production.

## Core Competencies
*   **Strategic Mindset:** Ability to connect complex technical decisions with ROI and business objectives.
*   **Solution Architecture:** Deep mastery of design patterns, frontend infrastructure, and modern technological ecosystems (React, Vite, Tailwind).
*   **Management & Influence:** Excellence in presenting solutions, negotiating scope, and coaching engineers to their highest potential.
*   **Clean Code Advocacy:** A relentless pursuit of maintainable codebases and the ability to mentor others in clean code principles at scale.
*   **Bilingual mandatory:** Advanced fluency in English and Spanish for stakeholder negotiation.

## Requirements
*   Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
*   **5+ years** of experience in technical leadership or engineering management roles.
*   Proven track record of working directly with international clients in a nearshore/offshore model.
*   Solid knowledge of Agile methodologies and cloud-integrated frontend architectures.
*   **Mandatory:** A portfolio demonstrating strategic ownership of large-scale, React-based enterprise applications.

*   USA Contractor / 100% Remote / Paid in USD.
*   Strategic influence over global accounts and high-growth technical roadmaps.
*   A mentorship-first culture where you help shape the leaders of tomorrow.
*   Flexible PTO and supportive health assistance programs.
