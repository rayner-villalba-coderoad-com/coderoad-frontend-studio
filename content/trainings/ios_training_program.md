## 🗺 Recommended Learning Paths

### 🚀 Fast Track (Full-time learner)
Complete one level every **6–8 weeks** by dedicating 20+ hours/week.

### 🏃 Standard Track (Working professional)
Complete one level every **3–4 months** by dedicating 8–10 hours/week.

### 🐢 Flexible Track (Casual learner)
Complete one level every **5–6 months** by dedicating 4–6 hours/week.
