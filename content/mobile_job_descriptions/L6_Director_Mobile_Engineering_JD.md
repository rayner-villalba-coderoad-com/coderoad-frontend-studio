# Director of Mobile Engineering (Level 6)

| Title: | Director of Mobile Engineering (L6) |
| --- | --- |
| Department: | Delivery / Mobile Engineering |
| Reports to: | VP, Engineering & Delivery |
| Employment Type: | Contractor |

### About CodeRoad

CodeRoad provides end-to-end software development services, helping businesses scale with ideal infrastructure solutions. From staff augmentation to dedicated IT teams and general software engineering, our nearshore technology services empower businesses to thrive in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

### About the Role

The **Director of Mobile Engineering (L6)** is responsible for leading and scaling CodeRoad’s mobile engineering organization. This role bridges strategic technical leadership, organizational growth, and delivery excellence, ensuring the success of high-complexity mobile initiatives across our client portfolio.

You will act as a strategic technical partner for our key clients, aligning their business needs with our mobile engineering capabilities. Your mission is to foster a culture of innovation and excellence, drive long-term technical vision for our mobile platforms, and strengthen CodeRoad’s reputation as a world-class technology advisor in the mobile space.

### 1️⃣ Technical Specialization

We are seeking a leader with deep expertise in **one** of the following stacks:

#### **React Native Director**
*   **Expertise:** Deep technical mastery of React Native and the broader JavaScript/TypeScript ecosystem.
*   **Architecture:** Expert knowledge of Modular, Clean, and Micro-Frontend mobile architectures.
*   **Strategy:** Proven ability to define and implement enterprise-grade CI/CD and automated testing strategies (Unit, E2E, Visual Regression).
*   **Native Depth:** Strong understanding of iOS and Android native internals and bridge management.
*   **Excellence:** Ability to set and enforce high standards for performance, security, and accessibility.

#### **Flutter Director**
*   **Expertise:** Deep technical mastery of Flutter and the Dart programming language.
*   **Architecture:** Expert knowledge of Clean Architecture, MVVM, and highly modularized Flutter codebases.
*   **Strategy:** Proven ability to define and implement enterprise-grade CI/CD and comprehensive automated testing strategies.
*   **Native Depth:** Strong understanding of platform channels and native OS integration.
*   **Excellence:** Ability to set and enforce high standards for UI performance, resource management, and multi-platform consistency.

### 2️⃣ Core Responsibilities

*   **Technical Vision:** Define and drive the multi-year technical roadmap for mobile platforms, staying ahead of industry trends.
*   **Strategic Partnership:** Act as the primary technical liaison for key accounts, ensuring technical solutions align with client business objectives.
*   **Team Scaling:** Lead the growth and maturity of the mobile engineering organization, focusing on recruitment, structural design, and talent development.
*   **Delivery Oversight:** Monitor delivery KPIs across teams, addressing risks proactively and ensuring consistency in Agile and engineering methodologies.
*   **Architectural Excellence:** Review and validate critical architectural decisions, ensuring scalability and reducing long-term technical debt.
*   **Engineering Culture:** Foster a culture of accountability, continuous learning, and innovation through mentorship and technical guidance.
*   **Pre-Sales Support:** Partner with Sales/Account management to provide technical credibility and guide solution designs for new opportunities.
*   **Stakeholder Influence:** Negotiate effectively with cross-functional leaders (Product, Design, Backend) to ensure mobile-first best practices.

### 3️⃣ Leadership & Soft Skills

*   **Strategic Thinking:** Ability to connect high-level technical direction with ROI and business outcomes.
*   **Mentorship:** Proven track record of developing senior engineers into Leads, Architects, and Managers.
*   **Communication:** Excellence in presenting technical solutions and thought leadership to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
*   **Relationship Management:** Ability to build trusted relationships internally and externally.
*   **Accountability:** High sense of ownership over the entire mobile organization's success and the "definition of done."

### 4️⃣ Growth Path: Your Journey at CodeRoad

Your success is measured by the maturity of the mobile organization and the strategic success of our mobile platform.

#### **First 90 Days: Assessment & Alignment**
*   **Success Indicators:** Successful assessment of current mobile engineering maturity; established baseline for delivery KPIs; identified key areas for cultural and technical improvement.

#### **First 6 Months: Organization Maturity**
*   **Success Indicators:** Implementation of improved engineering standards or structural changes; measurable growth in team retention and satisfaction; successful execution of a major platform-wide technical initiative.

#### **First 12 Months: Strategic Transformation**
*   **Success Indicators:** Recognized as a primary technical authority in the mobile industry; high client satisfaction (NPS) across the mobile portfolio; significant impact on team velocity and platform evolution toward "Director" and "VP" level organizational impact.

### Requirements

*   Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field (Advanced degree preferred).
*   Proven experience as a senior technical leader or director in a mobile-focused engineering organization.
*   Strong technical expertise across cross-platform mobile frameworks (React Native or Flutter).
*   Experience in nearshore/offshore client-facing models.
*   **Bilingual fluency (English and Spanish) is mandatory.**

### What you’ll love

*   **High-Level Impact:** Directly shape the future of a global mobile engineering division.
*   **Remote-First Excellence:** Work with a world-class team in a 100% remote, high-trust environment.
*   **Professional Growth:** Support for continuous training and evolution into VP levels.
*   **Global Projects:** Lead innovations for high-growth, industry-leading US-based companies.
