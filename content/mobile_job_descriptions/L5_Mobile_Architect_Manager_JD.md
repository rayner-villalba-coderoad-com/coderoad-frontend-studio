# Mobile Architect / Mobile Engineering Manager (Level 5)

| Title: | Mobile Architect / Engineering Manager (L5) |
| --- | --- |
| Department: | Delivery / Mobile Engineering |
| Reports to: | Engineering Director |
| Employment Type: | Contractor |

### About CodeRoad

CodeRoad provides end-to-end software development services, helping businesses scale with ideal infrastructure solutions. From staff augmentation to dedicated IT teams and general software engineering, our nearshore technology services empower businesses to thrive in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

### About the Role

The **Mobile Architect / Mobile Engineering Manager (L5)** at CodeRoad is a high-level technical leader focused on strategic business impact and client success. This role bridges the gap between commercial objectives and technical execution, acting as a strategic consultant for our mobile platform.

In this hybrid role, you will define the architectural vision for our mobile applications, lead and scale engineering teams, and ensure operational excellence in delivery. You are responsible for ensuring that the technical strategy aligns with the client’s business goals while fostering a high-performance engineering culture across multiple mobile initiatives.

### 1️⃣ Technical Specialization

We are seeking candidates specialized in **one** of the following stacks:

#### **React Native Mobile Architect / Manager**
*   **Expertise:** Expert-level experience architecting and scaling React Native applications at scale.
*   **Proficiency:** Mastery of JavaScript / TypeScript and advanced state management patterns.
*   **Architecture:** Deep understanding of Clean Architecture, MVVM, and Modularization.
*   **Integrations:** Advanced experience integrating native modules (iOS/Android) and bridge management.
*   **Platform Strategy:** Ability to define CI/CD pipelines, testing strategies (Unit, Integration, E2E), and release management processes.
*   **Performance:** Expert skills in profiling, debugging strategies, and performance optimization for mobile.

#### **Flutter Mobile Architect / Manager**
*   **Expertise:** Expert-level experience architecting and scaling Flutter applications at scale.
*   **Proficiency:** Mastery of Dart and reactive programming patterns.
*   **Architecture:** Deep understanding of Clean Architecture, MVVM, and Modularization.
*   **Platform Channels:** Expertise in developing and managing complex platform channels for native integrations.
*   **Platform Strategy:** Ability to define CI/CD pipelines, testing strategies (Unit, Widget, Integration, E2E), and release management processes.
*   **Performance:** Expert skills in profiling, debugging strategies, and performance optimization for mobile.

### 2️⃣ Core Responsibilities

*   **Architectural Strategy:** Define and evolve the core mobile architecture across all mobile applications, ensuring scalability, security, and maintainability.
*   **Engineering Standards:** Establish and enforce engineering standards, best practices, and development workflows across the mobile division.
*   **Technical Strategy:** Drive technical decisions that align with product development and long-term business strategy.
*   **Team Leadership:** Lead and mentor mobile engineers across all levels (L1-L4), overseeing the technical execution of one or more teams.
*   **Operational Excellence:** Ensure that mobile initiatives are delivered with high quality, on time, and within budget, meeting defined velocity and quality KPIs.
*   **Cross-Functional Collaboration:** Partner with Product, Design, Backend, and Platform teams to ensure a cohesive technical roadmaps and strategy.
*   **Strategic Pre-Sales:** Support sales and pre-sales activities by creating technical proposals, high-level estimates, and initial architecture designs.
*   **Technical Debt & Bottlenecks:** Identify and resolve systemic architectural bottlenecks and technical debt at a platform level.

### 3️⃣ Leadership & Management Skills

*   **Strategic Mindset:** Ability to connect technical decisions with ROI and the client's business objectives.
*   **Technical Advisory:** Ability to provide high-level consultancy to clients on mobile platform evolution and modernization.
*   **Coaching & Influence:** Excellence in mentoring leadership (L4s) and influencing engineering practices across the organization.
*   **Communication:** Fluent bilingual skills (English/Spanish) with excellence in presenting and negotiating with stakeholders.
*   **Ownership:** High sense of accountability for team outcomes and the overall success of the mobile platform.

### 4️⃣ Organizational Impact

*   **Platform Standards:** Responsibility for setting the "CodeRoad Mobile Standard," ensuring consistency across multiple projects and accounts.
*   **Scalable Processes:** Building and improving scalable team processes and engineering practices that can be replicated across pods.
*   **Long-term Evolution:** Collaborating with Engineering Leadership on the multi-year roadmap for the mobile platform.

### 5️⃣ Success Metrics and Growth Path

We support your growth into Director of Mobile Engineering or broader Engineering Leadership roles through strategic impact.

#### **First 90 Days: Strategic Alignment**
*   **Success Indicators:** Successful audit of existing mobile architectures and processes; established trust with client and internal leadership; delivery of a long-term mobile technical roadmap.

#### **First 6 Months: Execution & Scalability**
*   **Success Indicators:** Measurable improvement in team velocity and code health KPIs; successful delivery of a major mobile initiative; contribution to at least one account expansion or new proposal.

#### **First 12 Months: Platform Impact**
*   **Success Indicators:** Recognized as the primary technical authority for the mobile platform; high client satisfaction (NPS) and team retention scores; significant impact on operational efficiency across mobile teams.
*   **Path to Director:** Progression is based on your ability to scale technical operations across the entire mobile organization and drive global business success through technology.

### Requirements

*   5+ years of experience in technical leadership or engineering management roles.
*   Expert-level knowledge in mobile architectures (React Native or Flutter).
*   Proven experience working directly with international clients (Nearshore/Offshore).
*   **Bilingual fluency (English and Spanish) is mandatory.**
