### About CodeRoad

CodeRoad provides end-to-end software development services, helping businesses scale with ideal infrastructure solutions. From staff augmentation to dedicated IT teams and general software engineering, our nearshore technology services empower businesses to thrive in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

### About the Role

The **Lead Mobile Engineer (L4)** at CodeRoad is a tactical technical leader and the "Technical Anchor" for a mobile engineering team. You are responsible for the success of your POD, bridging the gap between high-level architectural requirements and daily technical execution.

This is a **hands-on leadership role** (approx. 60-70% coding and reviewing) where you will guide a diverse team of Senior, Intermediate, and Junior developers. You will ensure the delivery of high-quality, scalable mobile solutions while managing technical client expectations and mentoring your team to achieve their full potential.

### 1️⃣ Technical Specialization

We are seeking candidates specialized in **one** of the following stacks:

#### **React Native Lead Engineer**
*   **Expertise:** Advanced experience architecting and leading React Native projects.
*   **Proficiency:** Mastery of JavaScript / TypeScript and the React ecosystem.
*   **Mobile Architecture:** Expertise in MVVM, Clean Architecture, and Modular Architecture.
*   **Native Integration:** Experience with iOS/Android bridging and native module development.
*   **Optimization:** Deep knowledge of performance profiling and resource management.
*   **Testing:** Expertise in setting up and maintaining unit, integration, and E2E testing frameworks (Detox, Maestro).
*   **Tooling:** Mastery of Git advanced workflows and CI/CD pipelines (Fastlane, Github Actions).

#### **Flutter Lead Engineer**
*   **Expertise:** Advanced experience architecting and leading Flutter projects.
*   **Proficiency:** Mastery of Dart and reactive programming.
*   **Mobile Architecture:** Expertise in MVVM, Clean Architecture, and Modular Architecture.
*   **Platform Channels:** Proficiency in developing and managing complex platform channels.
*   **Optimization:** Deep knowledge of Flutter DevTools and performance optimization.
*   **Testing:** Expertise in setting up and maintaining unit, widget, and integration test suites.
*   **Tooling:** Mastery of Git advanced workflows and CI/CD pipelines (Fastlane, Github Actions).

### 2️⃣ Core Responsibilities

*   **Technical Leadership:** Lead the design, architecture, and development of mobile applications, ensuring alignment with product and business goals.
*   **Team Guidance:** Direct daily technical activities for the POD, unblocking team members and ensuring clear task distribution.
*   **Code Quality:** Own the local code review process, enforcing high standards for maintainability and scalability.
*   **Mentorship:** Actively coach engineers through pair programming and structured 1:1 sessions focusing on technical execution.
*   **Client Management:** Act as a technical point of contact for clients, translating business challenges into technical roadmaps and managing expectations.
*   **Project Delivery:** Tackle the most complex features and critical bugs while ensuring the team meet sprint commitments.
*   **Risk Management:** Identify and escalate potential technical risks or bottlenecks before they impact the delivery schedule.
*   **Talent Growth:** Participate in technical interviews and contribute to the growth of the mobile engineering team.

### 3️⃣ Leadership & Soft Skills

*   **Technical Excellence:** Mastery of the mobile tech stack and modern delivery workflows.
*   **Coaching & Mentorship:** Ability to foster a collaborative, high-performance environment.
*   **Buisness Acumen:** Ability to understand the "why" behind requirements from the client's perspective.
*   **Communication:** Fluent bilingual skills (English/Spanish) for international collaboration.
*   **Execution Focus:** A strong drive to deliver high-quality results while balancing speed and technical excellence.

### 4️⃣ Growth Path: Your Journey at CodeRoad

We support your evolution into Level 5 Architect or Managerial roles through strategic impact and team development.

#### **First 90 Days: Technical Anchoring**
*   **Success Indicators:** Successful integration as the POD's technical anchor; established trust with client stakeholders; full ownership of the POD's code review process.

#### **First 6 Months: Delivery Stability & Team Growth**
*   **Success Indicators:** Consistent on-time delivery of major project milestones; measurable growth in the autonomy of team members; successful identification and mitigation of technical risks.

#### **First 12 Months: Strategic Influence**
*   **Success Indicators:** Contribution to major architectural improvements across the account; high client and employee satisfaction scores (NPS/eNPS); recognized as a key technical leader in the mobile division.
*   **Path to Level 5:** Transition toward Architect (technical path) or Manager (people path) based on your strengths in solution design or organizational leadership.

### Requirements

*   Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
*   4+ years of experience in mobile engineering, with at least 1 year in a lead or "anchor" role.
*   Strong experience leading teams in Agile/Scrum environments.
*   **Bilingual fluency (English and Spanish) is mandatory.**
*   Proven ability to manage technical client expectations and team morale.
