# Junior Mobile Developer (Level 1 )

| Title: | Junior Mobile Developer (L1) |
| --- | --- |
| Department: | Delivery / Mobile Engineering |
| Reports to: | Intermediate / Senior Mobile Engineer |
| Employment Type: | Contractor |

### About CodeRoad

CodeRoad provides end-to-end software development services, helping businesses scale with ideal infrastructure solutions. From staff augmentation to dedicated IT teams and general software engineering, our nearshore technology services empower businesses to thrive in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

### About the Role

We are looking for an ambitious and curious **Junior Mobile Developer** to join our growing mobile engineering team. This role is designed for early-career developers who are passionate about building high-quality mobile experiences and are eager to grow within a production-grade environment.

At CodeRoad, we value an **"active learner"** mindset. You won’t just be completing tickets; you will be mentored to understand the "how" and the "why" behind every architectural decision. Your primary mission is to master the fundamentals of mobile development while contributing to real-world projects under the guidance of our senior engineering staff.

### 1️⃣ Technical Specialization

We are seeking candidates specialized in **one** of the following stacks:

#### **React Native Developer**
*   **React Native Fundamentals:** Solid understanding of components, hooks, and navigation.
*   **JavaScript / TypeScript:** Proficiency in modern JS/TS basics and clean coding practices.
*   **UI/UX:** Understanding of mobile UI components and responsive layout principles.
*   **Networking:** Experience integrating REST APIs using Fetch or Axios.
*   **Architecture:** Basic knowledge of mobile patterns such as MVC or MVVM.
*   **Tools:** Git fundamentals and experience with basic debugging (Chrome Debugger, React DevTools).

#### **Flutter Developer**
*   **Flutter Fundamentals:** Solid understanding of the widget tree and UI composition.
*   **Dart Basics:** Proficiency in Dart syntax and asynchronous programming (Futures/Streams).
*   **UI/UX:** Ability to build beautiful, fluid interfaces using Material and Cupertino widgets.
*   **Networking:** Experience integrating REST APIs using HTTP or Dio.
*   **Architecture:** Basic knowledge of mobile patterns such as MVC, MVVM, or Bloc/Provider.
*   **Tools:** Git fundamentals and experience with basic debugging (DevTools, Debug Console).

### 2️⃣ Core Expectations

*   **Execution:** Implement small features and resolve bug fixes under the mentorship of senior engineers.
*   **Standards:** Follow established coding standards, naming conventions, and team practices from day one.
*   **Quality:** Write clean, readable, and maintainable code.
*   **Learning:** Participate actively in code reviews, focusing on learning from feedback and applying it to future tasks.
*   **Testing:** Write basic unit tests for your contributions to ensure stability.
*   **Lifecycle:** Develop a fundamental understanding of the mobile application lifecycle and platform-specific behaviors.
*   **Collaboration:** Participate in Agile ceremonies (Stand-ups, Grooming, Retrospectives) and collaborate with backend, design, and QA teams.

### 3️⃣ Soft Skills

*   **Learning Mindset:** A strong willingness to learn, grow, and experiment with new mobile technologies.
*   **Coachability:** High openness to feedback and the ability to translate technical criticism into improved performance.
*   **Proactive Communication:** Clear and honest status updates; the ability to flag roadblocks early.
*   **Ownership:** A strong mindset for assigned tasks and a commitment to seeing them through to completion.
*   **Curiosity:** A passion for mobile development and a desire to understand the underlying mechanics of the platforms.

### 4️⃣ Growth Path: Your Journey at CodeRoad

We believe in structured mentorship and clear career evolution. Here is what success looks like in your first year:

#### **First 3 Months: Learning & Integration**
*   **Success Indicators:** Successful setup of the local development environment; completion of the onboarding program; delivery of initial UI tweaks and minor bug fixes; active participation in team meetings.
*   **Support:** Dedicated mentorship from a Senior Engineer and "buddy" support for daily queries.

#### **First 6 Months: Contribution & Skill Expansion**
*   **Success Indicators:** Implementing medium-complexity features with limited supervision; writing comprehensive unit tests for new code; a deeper understanding of the project’s specific architecture.
*   **Support:** Regular 1:1 sessions focused on technical growth and career mapping.

#### **First 12 Months: Autonomy & Evolution**
*   **Success Indicators:** Taking ownership of end-to-end feature modules; actively contributing to architectural discussions; demonstrating a high "Learning Velocity" and preparation for the Intermediate (L2) level transition.
*   **Support:** Advanced technical training opportunities and participation in cross-team knowledge-sharing sessions.

### Requirements

*   Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field (recently completed or in final year).
*   0–2 years of experience (relevant internships or academic projects are highly valued).
*   A portfolio of personal projects or active GitHub participation (React Native or Flutter).
*   **Upper-intermediate English (B2) or higher is mandatory for international collaboration.**
