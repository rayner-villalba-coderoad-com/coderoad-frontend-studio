### About CodeRoad

CodeRoad provides end-to-end software development services, helping businesses scale with ideal infrastructure solutions. From staff augmentation to dedicated IT teams and general software engineering, our nearshore technology services empower businesses to thrive in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

### About the Role

The **Intermediate Mobile Engineer (L2)** is a key contributor within our mobile engineering team, responsible for the consistent delivery of high-quality mobile applications. At this level, you have moved beyond foundational learning and are expected to handle well-defined technical challenges with increasing independence.

You are a solid technical executor who takes ownership of medium-sized features, writes maintainable and scalable code, and contributes to the overall engineering excellence of the team. While you still collaborate with Senior Engineers on complex architecture, you are expected to take initiative in problem-solving and feature implementation.

### 1️⃣ Technical Specialization

We are seeking candidates specialized in **one** of the following stacks:

#### **React Native Engineer**
*   **Experience:** Proven experience developing and deploying React Native applications.
*   **Proficiency:** Deep understanding of JavaScript/TypeScript and React hooks.
*   **State Management:** Expertise in at least one modern state management library (Redux, Zustand, Context API).
*   **APIs:** Strong experience integrating REST and/or GraphQL APIs.
*   **Architecture:** Solid understanding of mobile architecture patterns (MVC, MVVM) and Clean Architecture fundamentals.
*   **Performance:** Ability to identify and fix basic performance bottlenecks and memory leaks.
*   **DevOps:** Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines (Fastlane, Github Actions) and App Store/Play Store deployment.

#### **Flutter Engineer**
*   **Experience:** Proven experience developing and deploying Flutter applications.
*   **Proficiency:** Deep understanding of Dart and asynchronous programming patterns.
*   **State Management:** Expertise in at least one modern state management library (Bloc, Riverpod, Provider).
*   **APIs:** Strong experience integrating REST and/or GraphQL APIs.
*   **Architecture:** Solid understanding of mobile architecture patterns (MVC, MVVM) and Clean Architecture fundamentals.
*   **Performance:** Ability to identify and fix basic performance bottlenecks using Flutter DevTools.
*   **DevOps:** Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines (Fastlane, Github Actions) and App Store/Play Store deployment.

### 2️⃣ Core Expectations

*   **Ownership:** Design and implement medium-sized features from start to finish with limited supervision.
*   **Clean Code:** Write clean, maintainable, and testable mobile code following team standards and DRY principles.
*   **Code Quality:** Participate actively in code reviews, providing constructive feedback to peers and applying feedback to your own work.
*   **Testing:** Write comprehensive unit and widget/component tests to maintain reliable test coverage.
*   **Debugging:** Efficiently debug issues across the entire mobile stack (UI, state, networking).
*   **Collaboration:** Work effectively with backend engineers, designers, and product managers to translate requirements into technical solutions.
*   **Agile:** Participate in sprint planning, estimations, and technical design discussions.

### 3️⃣ Soft Skills

*   **Strong Ownership:** A mindset of accountability for your assigned features and deliverables.
*   **Effective Communication:** Ability to explain technical concepts clearly within cross-functional teams.
*   **Problem-Solving:** Proactive approach to identifying issues and proposing technical solutions.
*   **Feedback Mindset:** Openness to receiving and applying constructive criticism to improve both technical and soft skills.
*   **Scalability Mindset:** Commitment to writing code that is not only functional but also scalable and easy for others to maintain.

### 4️⃣ Growth Path: Your Journey at CodeRoad

We support your evolution toward becoming a Senior Engineer through increasing responsibility and technical depth.

#### **First 90 Days: Onboarding & Baseline Delivery**
*   **Success Indicators:** Full integration into the team’s Agile workflow; successful delivery of your first medium-sized feature; active contribution to peer code reviews.

#### **First 6 Months: Feature Ownership & Quality**
*   **Success Indicators:** Consistent delivery of high-quality features with minimal "returned" tickets; contribution to improving team documentation or common components; independent handling of complex bug fixes.

#### **First 12 Months: Technical Influence & Senior Path**
*   **Success Indicators:** Participation in initial architectural design sessions for new features; demonstrating technical leadership within specific modules; mentoring Junior developers (L1).
*   **Path to Senior:** Progression is based on your ability to handle increasingly complex architectural challenges and influence project-wide technical decisions.

### Requirements

*   Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent experience).
*   2–4 years of professional experience in mobile development.
*   Experience working within an Agile/Scrum framework.
*   Solid proficiency in Git workflows (feature branches, submodules, PRs).
*   **Upper-intermediate to Advanced English (B2/C1) is required for technical collaboration and documentation.**
