# Senior Mobile Engineer (Level 3)

### About CodeRoad

CodeRoad provides end-to-end software development services, helping businesses scale with ideal infrastructure solutions. From staff augmentation to dedicated IT teams and general software engineering, our nearshore technology services empower businesses to thrive in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

### About the Role

The **Senior Mobile Engineer (L3)** is a highly autonomous technical leader and subject matter expert within our mobile engineering division. At this level, your focus shifts from executing tasks to **owning outcomes** and driving architectural excellence across our projects.

You are expected to solve the most complex technical challenges, contribute to long-term architectural decisions, and ensure that our mobile applications are scalable, performant, and maintainable. While this is an individual contributor role, you will act as a technical mentor to Junior (L1) and Intermediate (L2) engineers, modeling best practices and influencing the engineering culture within your team.

### 1️⃣ Technical Specialization

We are seeking candidates specialized in **one** of the following stacks:

#### **React Native Senior Engineer**
*   **Expertise:** Advanced experience building complex, large-scale React Native applications.
*   **Proficiency:** Deep mastery of JavaScript / TypeScript and modern React patterns.
*   **Mobile Architecture:** Expertise in MVVM, Clean Architecture, and Modular Architecture patterns.
*   **Native Integration:** Experience with native bridge development for both iOS (Swift/Obj-C) and Android (Kotlin/Java) when required.
*   **Performance:** Advanced skills in profiling and optimizing app performance, memory management, and startup time.
*   **Testing:** Strong experience with automated testing strategies, including Jest for unit/integration and Detox or Maestro for E2E.
*   **DevOps:** Advanced knowledge of CI/CD pipelines (Fastlane, Github Actions) and platform-specific release management.

#### **Flutter Senior Engineer**
*   **Expertise:** Advanced experience building complex, large-scale Flutter applications.
*   **Proficiency:** Deep mastery of Dart and reactive programming patterns.
*   **Mobile Architecture:** Expertise in MVVM, Clean Architecture, and Modular Architecture patterns.
*   **Native Integration:** Proficiency with Platform Channels for native integration (iOS/Android).
*   **Performance:** Advanced skills in profiling using Flutter DevTools, optimizing frame rates, and resource management.
*   **Testing:** Strong experience with automated testing strategies, including widget, unit, and integration tests, as well as E2E testing (Integration Test package or similar).
*   **DevOps:** Advanced knowledge of CI/CD pipelines (Fastlane, Github Actions) and platform-specific release management.

### 2️⃣ Core Expectations

*   **Outcome Ownership:** Lead the design and end-to-end implementation of complex mobile systems and features.
*   **Architectural Leadership:** Contribute to mobile architecture decisions, ensuring scalability and long-term project health.
*   **Engineering Excellence:** Drive the adoption of best practices (Clean Code, TDD, Modularization) and identify/resolve technical debt proactively.
*   **Mentorship:** Elevate the team's technical bar by providing insightful code reviews and mentoring L1/L2 engineers.
*   **Collaboration:** Effectively translate technical constraints and possibilities into clear communication for Product, Design, and Backend stakeholders.
*   **Agile Advocacy:** Support planning and estimation, ensuring realistic timelines and high-quality technical delivery.

### 3️⃣ Leadership & Soft Skills

*   **Technical Leadership:** Ability to influence engineering practices and set high standards for technical execution.
*   **Strategic Thinking:** Ability to see the "big picture" and understand how technical decisions impact the business and the user experience.
*   **Communication:** Advanced English proficiency with the ability to articulate complex technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
*   **Accountability:** High sense of ownership over the "definition of done" and the overall success of the mobile team.
*   **Proactive Problem-Solving:** Ability to anticipate edge cases and performance bottlenecks before they become issues.

### 4️⃣ Growth Path: Your Journey at CodeRoad

We empower our Seniors to grow into Staff Engineers or Mobile Architects through technical influence and organizational impact.

#### **First 90 Days: Ownership & Influence**
*   **Success Indicators:** Successful delivery of a high-complexity feature; identification of at least one process improvement or technical optimization; established as a go-to person for junior team members.

#### **First 6 Months: Technical Direction**
*   **Success Indicators:** Leading architectural design for new project modules; measurable improvement in team code quality through mentoring; proactive management of technical debt.

#### **First 12 Months: Strategic Impact & Mentorship**
*   **Success Indicators:** Demonstrable impact on the growth of L1/L2 engineers; contribution to project-wide technical strategy; recognized as a key technical pillar for the mobile engineering division.
*   **Path to Staff/Architect:** Progression is based on your ability to solve multi-team technical challenges and influence the technical direction across the entire mobile organization.

### Requirements

*   Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent professional experience).
*   5+ years of professional experience in mobile development.
*   Proven track record of delivering high-impact technical solutions in an Agile environment.
*   Deep understanding of CI/CD, version control (Git), and modern delivery lifecycles.
*   **Advanced English proficiency (C1/C2) is mandatory for technical collaboration and documentation.**
