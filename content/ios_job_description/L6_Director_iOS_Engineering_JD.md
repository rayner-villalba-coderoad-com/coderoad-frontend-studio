# Director of iOS Engineering (Level 6)

| Title: | Director of iOS Engineering (Level 6) |
| --- | --- |
| Department: | iOS Mobile Engineering / Delivery |
| Reports to: | VP, Engineering & Delivery |
| Level: | L6 (Director) |

### About CodeRoad

CodeRoad provides end-to-end software development services, helping businesses scale with ideal infrastructure solutions. From staff augmentation to dedicated IT teams and general software engineering, our nearshore technology services empower businesses to thrive in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

### About the Role

The **Director of iOS Engineering** is responsible for leading and scaling CodeRoad’s mobile engineering organization, ensuring the successful delivery of world-class iOS solutions. This role bridges strategic technical leadership, organizational scaling, and delivery oversight, while fostering a culture of innovation and engineering excellence.

You will act as a strategic technical partner for our clients, aligning their high-level business needs with our advanced iOS engineering capabilities. Your focus is on fostering long-term partnerships, enabling client success, and strengthening CodeRoad’s reputation as a trusted technology advisor in the mobile space.

### Technical Stack (Strategic Oversight)

*   **Ecosystem Integrity:** High-level mastery of the **iOS Ecosystem** (Swift, SwiftUI, UIKit) and its integration with modern backend and cloud architectures.
*   **Architecture & Standards:** Expert guidance on mobile design patterns, modularization, and architectural best practices to ensure global product scalability.
*   **Quality Frameworks:** Definition and enforcement of organizational coding standards, design principles, and automated quality frameworks.
*   **CI/CD & Delivery:** Strategic oversight of mobile delivery lifecycles, automated testing strategies, and App Store distribution at scale.

### Key Responsibilities

#### 1. Strategic Client Partnership
*   **Technical Liaison:** Act as the primary technical contact for key clients, understanding their business objectives and aligning iOS solutions with their technical roadmaps.
*   **Thought Leadership:** Build trust-based relationships through proactive engagement and providing strategic mobile engineering advice.
*   **Pre-Sales Support:** Support the Sales team in proposals and PoCs by providing technical credibility and high-level mobile solution guidance.

#### 2. Solution Advisory & Technical Leadership
*   **Expert Guidance:** Provide strategic direction on technology choices, mobile architectures, and industry trends to ensure long-term product viability.
*   **Architectural Validation:** Review and validate critical architectural decisions across all iOS projects within the organization.
*   **Standard Setting:** Define and enforce CodeRoad’s iOS engineering standards and mobile quality frameworks.

#### 3. Delivery Oversight
*   **Operational Excellence:** Collaborate with Project/Delivery Managers and Leads to ensure on-time, high-quality project execution.
*   **KPI Monitoring:** Monitor delivery health metrics (velocity, quality, utilization) and address organizational risks proactively.
*   **Agile Consistency:** Drive consistency in mobile Agile practices and engineering methodologies across all Apple-focused teams.
*   **Talent Oversight:** Actively participate in final technical and cultural interviews for candidates joining the iOS studio.

#### 4. People Development & Leadership
*   **Mentorship:** Coach and mentor Studio Leads, Architects, and Senior Engineers, fostering their professional growth toward leadership roles.
*   **Career Frameworks:** Partner with HR/COO to implement and refine career paths for mobile engineering roles.
*   **Culture Building:** Foster a strong culture of accountability, clean code commitment, and continuous learning.

#### 5. Continuous Improvement
*   **Process Optimization:** Identify opportunities to improve client-facing delivery and internal mobile engineering processes (automation, tooling).
*   **Knowledge Sharing:** Promote cross-account knowledge sharing and ensure adoption of new Swift features and mobile methodologies.

### Growth Path & Success Measures

Your success is tied directly to the organizational health and delivery stability:

*   **Client & Employee Satisfaction (NPS/eNPS):** Maintaining high technical satisfaction scores across clients and the internal iOS team.
*   **Technical Stability:** Reducing technical debt and preventing critical failures in production at an organizational level.
*   **Success in 1 Year:**
    *   **3-6 Months:** Stabilizing pod performance; establishing leadership mentorship cycles; aligning organizational standards.
    *   **1 Year:** Scaling the iOS organization; driving strategic innovative mobile initiatives; measurably elevating the technical seniority of the studio.

#### Mentorship & Professional Evolution
At Level 6, you will receive executive mentorship focused on business strategy, organizational design, and scaling global engineering operations.

### Core Competencies & Soft Skills

*   **Strategic Partnership:** Ability to connect complex technical mobile decisions with ROI and client business objectives.
*   **Relationship Management:** Excellence in building trusted relationships and negotiating scope with stakeholders.
*   **Business Acumen:** Deep understanding of industry trends and connecting mobile solutions to long-term client success.
*   **Influential Leadership:** Ability to inspire excellence and foster a high-performance engineering culture.

### Requirements

*   Bachelor’s or Advanced Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.
*   8+ years of engineering experience, with 3+ years in senior technical leadership or management roles.
*   Strong technical expertise across the Apple mobile ecosystem and modern delivery methodologies.
*   Proven experience working directly with international clients in a nearshore/offshore model.
*   **Conversational fluency in English and Spanish is preferred** for global collaboration.

*   USA Contractor / 100% Remote.
*   Competitive Pay in USD.
*   Holidays Off & Paid Time Off.
*   Leading a world-class team of iOS engineers and shaping the future of mobile delivery.
