### About CodeRoad

CodeRoad provides end-to-end software development services, helping businesses scale with ideal infrastructure solutions. From staff augmentation to dedicated IT teams and general software engineering, our nearshore technology services empower businesses to thrive in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

### About the Role

The **Lead iOS Engineer (Level 4)** at CodeRoad is a tactical technical leader responsible for the success of a specific mobile POD or project team. Acting as the **"Technical Anchor,"** this role bridges the gap between high-level architectural requirements and daily mobile execution.

This is a hands-on leadership role (approx. 60-70% coding/reviewing) focused on guiding a diverse team of Senior, Intermediate, and Junior developers. You ensure the team delivers high-quality, performant iOS applications on time, while translating business objectives into technical roadmaps. You combine technical excellence with team mentorship and client advisory, ensuring the overall health of the mobile project.

### Technical Stack

*   **Language Master:** Mastery of **Swift** and modern language features (Concurrency, Swift Package Manager).
*   **UI Ecosystem:** Deep proficiency in **SwiftUI** and/or **UIKit**, with an emphasis on modular UI design.
*   **Networking & Data:** Expert integration of **REST APIs** and architectural standards for data persistence.
*   **Architecture:** Strategic application of mobile patterns (**MVC**, **MVVM**, **Clean Architecture**) and system modularization.
*   **DevOps & Git:** Expert knowledge of **Git** workflows, CI/CD pipelines (Xcode Cloud, Bitrise, Fastlane), and App Store distribution.

### Key Responsibilities

#### 1. Strategic Account & Client Management
*   **Technical Partnership:** Act as the primary technical point of contact for clients, translating business challenges into actionable iOS technical roadmaps.
*   **Client Advisor:** Proactively identify technological improvement opportunities (e.g., refactoring legacy code, performance optimization) within current accounts.
*   **Expectation Management:** Maintain transparency with client stakeholders regarding technical risks, timelines, and architectural decisions.

#### 2. POD Leadership & Technical Mentorship
*   **Team Guidance:** Direct the daily technical activities of the mobile POD, ensuring clear task distribution and unblocking team members.
*   **Mentorship:** Actively coach Senior, Intermediate, and Junior developers through pair programming and constructive 1:1 sessions.
*   **Code Quality:** Own the local code review process, ensuring 100% adherence to CodeRoad’s and the client’s engineering standards (Clean Code, SOLID).
*   **Team Health:** Monitor team morale, proactively identifying burnout risks and fostering a collaborative, low-friction environment.

#### 3. Project Delivery & Execution
*   **Tactical Planning:** Break down complex features into manageable technical tasks and provide realistic estimations.
*   **Sprint Ownership:** Collaborate with Project Managers to ensure the sprint backlog is technically sound and achievable.
*   **Hands-on Contribution:** Lead by example by tackling the most complex architectural components or critical system-wide bugs.
*   **Talent Acquisition:** Participate in technical and cultural interviews for candidates joining the mobile team.

#### 4. Incipient Strategy & Architecture
*   **Implementation Design:** Design the technical components of features based on high-level architecture provided by the Architect (L5).
*   **Risk Mitigation:** Identify and escalate potential technical risks or bottlenecks before they impact the delivery schedule.

### Growth Path & Success Measures

Your success is tied to the health of the partnership and the growth of your team:

*   **Client & Team Satisfaction (NPS/eNPS):** Maintaining high satisfaction scores from both clients and your internal team.
*   **Sprint Predictability:** Consistently meeting sprint commitments with high-quality, stable code.
*   **Team Growth:** Measurable improvement in the autonomy and skill level of the Junior/Intermediate developers in your POD.
*   **3-6 Month Success:**
    *   **3 Months:** Stabilizing pod velocity; establishing a clear mentorship rhythm; delivering initial architectural improvements.
    *   **6 Months:** Identifying account expansion opportunities; scaling team capacity; preparing for progression to **iOS Architect (L5)**.

#### Progression to Architect (L5)
The path to L5 focuses on solution architecture, multi-account strategy, and high-level cross-account leadership.

### Core Competencies & Soft Skills

*   **Technical Excellence:** Mastery of the iOS tech stack and modern delivery lifecycles.
*   **Empathetic Leadership:** Ability to guide others and foster a high-performance, inclusive team environment.
*   **Business Acumen:** An emerging ability to understand the "why" behind technical requirements from a client's perspective.
*   **Extreme Ownership:** A "get-things-done" mindset while maintaining the highest engineering standards.

### Requirements

*   Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
*   4+ years of experience in iOS engineering, with at least 1 year in a lead or "Technical Anchor" role.
*   Strong experience in Agile/Scrum environments and mobile distribution pipelines.
*   Proven ability to mentor developers and manage large-scale code quality.
*   **Bilingual fluency (English and Spanish) is mandatory** for global collaboration.
