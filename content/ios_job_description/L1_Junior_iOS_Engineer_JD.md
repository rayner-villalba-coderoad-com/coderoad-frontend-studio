From staff augmentation to dedicated IT teams and general software engineering, our nearshore technology services empower businesses to thrive in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

### About the Role

The **Junior iOS Engineer (Level 1)** is an entry-level professional focused on developing foundational technical skills within a high-performance mobile production environment. At this level, your primary mission is to learn, observe, and contribute to well-defined, low-complexity tasks under the direct supervision of a Lead or Senior Engineer.

This role is ideal for an aspiring iOS developer who is highly coachable, possesses a strong theoretical foundation in Swift, and is eager to apply their knowledge to real-world mobile projects. You are expected to be an "active learner": someone who doesn't just complete a task but seeks to understand the "how" and "why" behind mobile architecture and performance to accelerate their path toward professional mastery.

### Technical Stack

*   **Language Master:** Foundational proficiency in **Swift**.
*   **UI Ecosystem:** Basic understanding of **SwiftUI** and/or **UIKit**.
*   **Networking:** Familiarity with **REST API** integration patterns.
*   **Architecture:** Basic understanding of mobile patterns (**MVC**, **MVVM**).
*   **Version Control:** Fundamental knowledge of **Git** (branching, commits, PRs).

### Key Responsibilities

#### 1. Guided Execution & Delivery
*   **Feature Contribution:** Execute specific technical tickets (e.g., UI tweaks, simple bug fixes, or small feature updates) as defined by the Technical Lead.
*   **Clean Code:** Write clean, readable, and maintainable code following team standards and SOLID principles.
*   **Testing:** Apply basic unit testing knowledge (XCTest) to maintain code quality.
*   **Environment Management:** Successfully set up and maintain local Xcode development or testing environments following team documentation.

#### 2. Team Integration & Learning
*   **Code Reviews:** Participate in code reviews to learn best practices and receive constructive feedback.
*   **Shadowing:** Participate in pair programming sessions and shadow senior team members to understand complex system interactions.
*   **Collaboration:** Work closely with backend and design teams to understand how your work fits into the overall platform.
*   **Active Listening:** Attend all Agile ceremonies (Stand-ups, Grooming, Retrospectives) to learn how mobile projects are managed.

#### 3. Reliability & Communication
*   **Proactive Communication:** Provide honest and clear updates on progress, ensuring roadblocks are communicated immediately.
*   **Ownership Mindset:** Take responsibility for assigned tasks and see them through to completion.
*   **Documentation:** Assist in keeping project documentation up to date, using it to solidify your understanding of the app lifecycle and performance.

### Growth Path & Success Measures

We believe in transparency regarding your evolution as an engineer. Your success in the first 6 months is measured by:

*   **Learning Velocity:** The speed at which you master our iOS tech stack and internal practices.
*   **Coachability:** Your ability to take critical feedback as a gift and apply it to future deliverables.
*   **Reliability:** Consistency in meeting deadlines and active participation in team discussions.
*   **3-6 Month Success:**
    *   **3 Months:** Environment fully optimized; first production PRs merged; familiar with project coding standards.
    *   **6 Months:** Completing medium-complexity tasks with less supervision; contributing meaningfully to technical grooming.

#### Mentorship Structure
Every Junior is paired with a **Senior/Lead Mentor**. You will have weekly 1:1 technical coaching sessions, pair-programming deep dives, and a clear path toward becoming a **Mid-Level Mobile Engineer**.

### Core Competencies & Soft Skills

*   **High Coachability:** Openness to feedback and a desire to pivot based on senior guidance.
*   **Curiosity:** A continuous learning attitude and a drive to research solutions before asking for help.
*   **Clear Communication:** Ability to explain technical logic clearly in an international environment.
*   **Team Mindset:** A helpful attitude and willingness to support the team’s collective goals.

### Requirements

*   Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field (recently completed or in final year).
*   0–2 years of experience in iOS development (academic projects, internships, or personal apps are highly valued).
*   Understanding of the iOS app lifecycle and performance basics.
*   **B2+ English proficiency** is mandatory for collaboration in our international environment.
*   A portfolio of personal projects or active GitHub profile is a significant plus.
