### About CodeRoad

CodeRoad provides end-to-end software development services, helping businesses scale with ideal infrastructure solutions. From staff augmentation to dedicated IT teams and general software engineering, our nearshore technology services empower businesses to thrive in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

### About the Role

The **Architect / Manager of iOS Engineering** at CodeRoad is a high-level technical leader focused on business impact and client success. This role is a hybrid that combines solution architecture, account management from a technical perspective, and team leadership within the day-to-day project environment.

Your mission is to ensure that the technical vision for our mobile applications aligns with the client’s commercial objectives, acting as a strategic consultant and ensuring operational excellence in delivery. You are responsible for the overall architectural health of major mobile projects and the professional growth of the engineering talent within your scope.

### Technical Stack (Specialized Mastery)

*   **Language & Platform Expert:** Deep mastery of **Swift**, **SwiftUI**, and **UIKit**, with an emphasis on performance, responsiveness, and memory management.
*   **Architectural Strategy:** Expert-level knowledge of design patterns (**MVC**, **MVVM**, **Clean Architecture**) and modularization strategies for large-scale iOS apps.
*   **Networking & Infrastructure:** Strategic integration of **REST APIs**, cloud-native mobile architectures, and secure data persistence.
*   **Ecosystem & Tools:** Mastery of **Git**, CI/CD pipelines (Xcode Cloud, Bitrise, Bitbucket Pipelines), and the full App Store Connect/TestFlight lifecycle.

### Key Responsibilities

#### 1. Strategic Account & Client Management
*   **Technical Partnership:** Act as the primary technical point of contact for assigned clients, translating complex business challenges into mobile technical roadmaps.
*   **Client Advisor:** Proactively identify expansion or technological improvement opportunities within current accounts, collaborating with stakeholders to present value propositions.
*   **Expectation Management:** Manage technical expectations of client stakeholders, ensuring transparency regarding risks, timelines, and architectural decisions.

#### 2. Solution Architecture & Technical Oversight
*   **Architectural Leadership:** Define and oversee the architecture of assigned iOS projects, ensuring they are scalable, secure, and maintainable.
*   **Quality Assurance:** Ensure CodeRoad’s engineering standards (Clean Code, SOLID) are rigorously applied through critical reviews and periodic architectural audits.
*   **Risk Mitigation:** Identify technical bottlenecks early and execute contingency plans to avoid delivery impacts.

#### 3. Delivery Excellence & Execution
*   **Team Leadership:** Lead the technical execution of one or more mobile PODs, ensuring optimal workflow efficiency and quality KPIs.
*   **1:1 Coaching:** Conduct scheduled, mandatory 1:1 coaching sessions focusing on execution, technical growth, and professional development.
*   **Agile Orchestration:** Collaborate with Project Managers to plan sprints, estimate efforts, and prioritize the technical backlog according to business needs.
*   **Talent Acquisition:** Participate in final technical and cultural interviews for candidates joining the mobile teams.

#### 4. Technical Pre-Sales & Advisory
*   **Solution Design:** Support Sales in creating technical proposals and initial architecture designs for new prospects.
*   **Advisory Services:** Provide high-level technical consultancy to clients seeking mobile modernization or adoption of new technologies.

### Growth Path & Success Measures

Your success is directly tied to the health of the partnership and the growth of the iOS talent:

*   **Client & Employee Satisfaction (NPS/eNPS):** Maintaining high technical satisfaction scores from both clients and team members.
*   **Account Expansion:** Contributing to account retention and identifying new business opportunities.
*   **Predictable Delivery:** Successfully meeting project milestones on-time and with high technical quality.
*   **1 Year Success:**
    *   **3-6 Months:** Establishing a high-performing pod culture; delivering a major architectural refactor or new project launch; stabilizing client technical communications.
    *   **1 Year:** Driving strategic mobile innovation across accounts; measurably improving team seniority through coaching; preparing for progression to **Director of iOS Engineering (L6)**.

#### Mentorship & Leadership Progression
The path to L6 focuses on organizational strategy, multi-studio leadership, and shaping the company's global engineering standards.

### Core Competencies & Soft Skills

*   **Strategic Mindset:** Ability to connect complex technical decisions with ROI and client business objectives.
*   **High Coachability:** Lead by example in openness to feedback and continuous learning.
*   **Proactive Communication:** Excellence in presenting technical solutions and negotiating scope.
*   **Extreme Ownership:** A "get-things-done" attitude with a relentless focus on engineering excellence and team morale.

### Requirements

*   Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field (or equivalent practical experience).
*   5+ years of experience in technical leadership or engineering management roles within the iOS ecosystem.
*   Proven experience working directly with international clients (Nearshore/Offshore model).
*   Deep knowledge of mobile design patterns, cloud architectures, and Agile methodologies.
*   **Bilingual fluency (English and Spanish) is mandatory.**
