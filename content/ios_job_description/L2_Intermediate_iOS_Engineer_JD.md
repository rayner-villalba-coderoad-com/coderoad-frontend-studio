### About the Role

The **Intermediate iOS Engineer (Level 2)** is a key contributor within a POD, responsible for the consistent delivery of high-quality technical tasks and features. At Level 2, the role moves beyond the foundational learning stage and is expected to handle well-defined technical challenges with increasing independence.

The primary focus of the role is high-quality execution. You are expected to write clean, maintainable code, participate actively in code reviews, and collaborate effectively with your peers and mentors. While you still receive guidance on complex architectural decisions from Senior Engineers, you are expected to take initiative in solving problems and managing your own time to meet sprint goals.

### Technical Stack

*   **Language Master:** Strong proficiency in **Swift**.
*   **UI Ecosystem:** Practical experience with **SwiftUI** and/or **UIKit**.
*   **Networking:** Integration of **REST APIs** following architectural patterns.
*   **Architecture:** Solid understanding of mobile patterns (**MVC**, **MVVM**).
*   **Version Control:** Proficiency in **Git** fundamentals (branching models, PR etiquette).

### Key Responsibilities

#### 1. Technical Implementation
*   **Task Execution:** Deliver features, bug fixes, and unit tests that are performant, secure, and aligned with project standards.
*   **Deliverable Quality:** Adhere to established best practices (SOLiD, DRY principles, internal style guides) to ensure the long-term health of the codebase.
*   **Testing & Validation:** Own the quality of your work by implementing unit tests (XCTest) before handing off for peer review.
*   **App Lifecycle:** Demonstrate a clear understanding of the iOS app lifecycle and fundamental performance optimization.

#### 2. Team Contribution & Collaboration
*   **Active Participation:** Engage in daily stand-ups, grooming sessions, and retrospectives, providing clear updates on progress and blockers.
*   **Peer Reviews:** Actively participate in the code review process, both by receiving feedback with a growth mindset and by providing constructive initial reviews for peers.
*   **Documentation:** Contribute to technical documentation and Readmes to ensure team knowledge remains shared and accessible.
*   **Cross-Functional Collaboration:** Partner with backend and design teams to ensure seamless feature integration and high-quality UX.

#### 3. Continuous Learning & Growth
*   **Skill Evolution:** Actively work to master the tools and technologies used within your POD, seeking out learning opportunities and mentorship.
*   **Process Adoption:** Consistently follow the team’s Git workflows, CI/CD processes, and communication protocols.
*   **Initiative:** Take ownership of small-to-medium modules, demonstrating an ability to research and implement solutions with minimal guidance on standard tasks.

### Growth Path & Success Measures

We believe in transparency regarding your evolution. Your success is measured by:

*   **Predictability:** The ability to estimate tasks accurately and deliver them consistently within sprint timeframes.
*   **Technical Growth:** Demonstrable improvement in technical depth and the ability to handle increasingly complex architectural tasks.
*   **Team Synergy:** Positive contributions to the POD's velocity and a collaborative attitude toward unblocking teammates.
*   **Success in 3-6 Months:**
    *   **3 Months:** Fully integrated into the sprint cycle; delivering features independently; active participant in technical grooming.
    *   **6 Months:** Mastering complex modules; identifying technical debt; preparing for progression toward Senior roles.

#### Mentorship & Progression
Every Intermediate Engineer has access to **Senior/Lead Mentorship**. We provide a structured path toward becoming a **Senior iOS Engineer**, focused on leadership, architectural ownership, and technical excellence.

### Core Competencies & Soft Skills

*   **High Coachability:** Openness to feedback and rapid implementation of senior guidance.
*   **Strong Ownership:** A "can-do" attitude and a proactive approach to identifying and solving system issues.
*   **Proactive Communication:** Clear and timely updates on progress, risks, and blockers.
*   **Commitment to Excellence:** A deep focus on clean code, best practices, and continuous learning.

### Requirements

*   Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent practical experience).
*   2–4 years of professional experience in iOS development.
*   Experience working within an Agile/Scrum framework.
*   Solid understanding of standard development environments and iOS deployment basics (TestFlight, App Store Connect).
*   **B2+ English proficiency** (written and verbal) for collaborating with a global engineering team.
