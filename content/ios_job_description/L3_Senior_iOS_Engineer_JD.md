### About the Role

The **Senior iOS Engineer (Level 3)** is a highly autonomous contributor and a subject matter expert within the mobile engineering discipline. At Level 3, the focus shifts from "executing tasks" to **"owning outcomes."** You are responsible for ensuring that our iOS applications are not only functional but also scalable, maintainable, and aligned with market-leading performance standards.

In this role, you will solve complex technical problems, contribute to high-level architectural discussions, and serve as a technical beacon for the team. While you still receive strategic guidance from Lead Engineers, you are expected to drive the "how" of technical execution, modeling best practices in Clean Code and architectural integrity for your peers.

### Technical Stack

*   **Language Master:** Expert-level proficiency in **Swift**.
*   **UI Ecosystem:** Deep understanding of **SwiftUI** and/or **UIKit**, including complex animations and custom component design.
*   **Networking & Data:** Advanced integration of **REST APIs** and mastery of offline data strategies.
*   **Architecture:** Mastery of mobile patterns (**MVC**, **MVVM**, **Clean Architecture**) and modularization strategies.
*   **Version Control:** Expert knowledge of **Git** (git-flow, conflict resolution, CI/CD integration).

### Key Responsibilities

#### 1. Technical Execution & Ownership
*   **End-to-End Delivery:** Own the lifecycle of complex iOS features or architectural components from requirement analysis to production deployment.
*   **Advanced Problem Solving:** Tackle the most challenging technical hurdles, proactively identifying edge cases and performance bottlenecks before they reach production.
*   **Standards Champion:** Implementation and advocacy for best practices (TDD, Clean Code, SOLID) to ensure a healthy, regression-free codebase.
*   **App Lifecycle & Performance:** Senior-level understanding of memory management, concurrency, and app store deployment lifecycles.

#### 2. Collaboration & Team Influence
*   **Deep Peer Reviews:** Provide insightful, technical feedback during code reviews to elevate the team's overall engineering quality.
*   **Mentorship:** Support the growth of Level 1 and Level 2 engineers through knowledge sharing, pair programming, and technical documentation.
*   **Cross-Functional Communication:** Effectively translate technical mobile constraints and possibilities to Product Owners, Designers, and Backend teams.
*   **Openness to Feedback:** Maintaining a high degree of coachability while collaborating with Lead Engineers on strategic roadmaps.

#### 3. Quality & Continuous Improvement
*   **System Health:** Maintain and improve the stability of the iOS environment, focusing on performance optimization, security, and scalability.
*   **Innovation:** Research and propose new tools, libraries (e.g., Combine, Swift Concurrency), or methodologies to improve POD efficiency.
*   **Documentation:** Ensure technical designs and infrastructure changes are documented for long-term project maintainability.

### Growth Path & Success Measures

We believe in radical transparency regarding your professional evolution. Your success is measured by:

*   **Autonomy:** The ability to take high-level requirements and turn them into high-quality technical realities with minimal supervision.
*   **Technical Debt Management:** Proactive identification and resolution of technical debt within the iOS codebase.
*   **Review Impact:** Demonstrable improvement in the quality of team output resulting from your technical guidance.
*   **Success in 3-6 Months:**
    *   **3 Months:** Owning a major functional module; delivering complex features independently; actively mentoring L1/L2 peers.
    *   **6 Months:** Leading architectural refactors; identifying strategic technical initiatives; preparing for progression toward **Lead iOS Engineer**.

#### Mentorship & Leadership Path
As a Senior Engineer, you are on a fast track to **Lead iOS Engineer**. Your evolution will focus on team leadership, architectural strategy, and driving engineering culture across multiple PODs.

### Core Competencies & Soft Skills

*   **Strong Ownership Mindset:** You don't just "do tasks"; you ensure the product's success and the team's health.
*   **High Coachability:** Openness to feedback from Leads and a commitment to continuous learning.
*   **Proactive Communication:** Clear, authoritative, and timely communication with stakeholders.
*   **Clean Code Commitment:** A relentless focus on best practices and maintainable engineering.

### Requirements

*   Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent practical experience).
*   5+ years of professional experience in iOS development.
*   Proven track record of delivering high-impact technical solutions in Agile/Scrum environments.
*   Deep understanding of CI/CD, mobile automation, and App Store Connect lifecycles.
*   **Advanced English proficiency** (written and verbal) is mandatory.
